GoBlue

A complete Cellphone based e-commerce solution.

Local Service - Direct Checkout (ie Store Checkout, Parking Checkout)

Customer enters into a checkout room. The RFID reader automatically reads all the RFID label (price) tags on the products. Meanwhile the RFID reader calculates total price from the RFID tags attached to each products and informs the cell phone about the total. The customer being notified of the payment process takes his cell phone out of his pocket, completes the easy steps on his cell phone and makes the payment. Once the payment is authorized, the door in front automatically opens and the customer leaves.
